User Experience of Streaming Platforms

Netflix

Netflix offers an intuitive interface with personalized recommendations, seamless navigation, and detailed categories for content discovery.

Hulu

Hulu provides a clean and simple UI, with a focus on current TV shows and ease of access to recently aired episodes.

Disney+

Disney+ features a visually appealing interface with easy-to-navigate menus and dedicated sections for popular franchises.

Amazon Prime Video

Prime Video provides a broad content library, though its interface can feel cluttered, with less intuitive navigation.

HBO Max

HBO Max delivers a modern design with smooth streaming, though some users note occasional search inefficiencies.
